DON'T WASTE MORE — WASTE LESS

Wasteless will help make cooking fun and exciting as well as making sure nothing goes to waste!

Keep Track

With the wasteless app you can keep track of what products that are stored in your refrigerator and when their expiration date is due.

Easy recipees

Wasteless will automatically find recipes based on what ingredients that are stored in your refrigerator.

Cook with friends

With WasteLess you have the opportunity to share your refrigerator with other users and make suggestions for food with others. By adding users to your friend list you will get suggestions for dishes based on what your friends and you have in the fridge.
